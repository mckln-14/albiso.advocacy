About the Advocacy

Poverty refers to the deprivation of essential human needs. "Hope Beyond Hunger" aims to address poverty by providing opportunities, education, and sustainable programs to uplift individuals and communities.

Key Projects

Job Opportunity Seminars

Partnering with TESDA to train individuals in marketable skills such as massage therapy and other trades, empowering them with livelihood opportunities.

Education Support

Organizing tutoring programs, donating school supplies, and collaborating with organizations to offer scholarships for underprivileged students.

Community Outreach

Running feeding programs and donation drives, especially during natural calamities, to provide immediate relief to affected communities.
